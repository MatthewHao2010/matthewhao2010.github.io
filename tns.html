Teaching at Home

When I started my teaching at home at the age of 4, my grandmother and parents were my first students. Teaching at home for several years, they supported me unwaveringly, which shaped me into a confident and expressive person. Their encouragement inspired me to volunteer to teach in remote villages and record online teaching videos. These experiences have strengthened my belief that helping others thrive is one of my life missions, and I will continue to use my support to influence more people's lives! I hope you enjoy the videos!

Volunteer Teaching

My mother is an education expert who selflessly helps others turn their lives positive, which has shaped me into a confident and selfless person.Their encouragement and impact inspired me to volunteer to teach in remote villages, which started from 2018. After 3 years, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I switched to online teaching, recording videos and uploading them to video platforms. These experiences have strengthened my belief that helping others thrive is one of my life missions, and I will continue to use my support to influence more people's lives! Thank you for watching!
